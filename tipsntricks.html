Tips & Tricks


Tips:

  1. Don't cut your fingers off.
  2. Use quality wood.
  3. Always use the right tools for the job.
  4. Don't use a machine until you know how to use it safely.
  5. Always have fun!
  6. Wear safety goggles.
  7. Take good care of your tools.
  8. Keep dangerous tools away from children.
  9. Don't try to hit the nail in the wood with one hit.
  10. Don't forget to buy our wood!
